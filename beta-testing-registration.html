Due to the way our servers work, unfortunately we require you to enter your login details so we can check if your account is eligible for beta testing.

To prevent abuse, there are some requirements you need to meet before you can join in to beta testing.

Your account needs:

 

- to be level 20 or higher;

- at least some world locks or gems;

- to be actively playing in the game.

 

When you get accepted, you'll receive these following rewards on the main servers:

 

- Road to glory and 100K gems instantly

- 5 months worth of ITEM OF THE MONTH

- 10 diamond locks (for each review)

- Custom name title and custom skin colors

- Super supporter

 

You'll also get an email with access codes to beta testing private servers with more information.
