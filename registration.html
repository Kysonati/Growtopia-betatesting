To prevent abuse, there are some requirements your account needs to meet before you can join in to beta testing.

Your account needs to:

 

- be level 20 or higher;

- have at least some world locks or gems;

- have at least 50 hours of playtime;

- still be actively playing the game.

- Road to glory and 100K gems instantly;

- 5 months worth of Item Of The Month;

- 10 diamond locks (for each review);

- Custom name title and custom skin colors;

- Super supporter;

- And other cool stuff!

 

You'll also get an email with access codes to beta testing private servers with more information.
